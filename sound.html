I make music as Theydon Boys, as part of 0E20F2, and I work with sound design, experimental composition and improvisation.

  • 0E20F2 - PANCREAS DENIAL [2021]

    PANCREAS DENIAL, the debut EP of Berlin-based 0E20F2, is a tempting invitation for a walk down the spiral of madness. The very texture of sound is undone through a nightmarish Burroughsian process of dissociation. In a disorientating flurry of sonic fragments and occult references, we progress through diverse realities towards a point where reality itself is only one of many answers.

    "Everything, in the end, becomes true."

    Cassette available via Industrial Coast
