I am a London-based artist, musician and programmer, currently studying Music Computing at Goldsmiths, University of London. Having grown up in the peculiar post-industrial town of Partizánske in Slovakia, and later spent several years studying for the International Baccalaureate in the decadence and chaos of Bratislava, I am now striving to build upon these experiences and develop my artistic signature in the United Kingdom. My work includes musical compositions, audiovisual installations, live performances and computer applications ▶

Despite being trained in classical guitar and, later on, jazz and free improvisation, my main musical interest is electronic music and computer art in general. I am mostly interested in making pieces that are free of framework limitations set by the traditional notion of exposition in a gallery or a concert. Furthermore, I have been exploring the collective nature of human society as a tool for creation of art. This leads to the vision of open & organic art – public, evolving, indeterminate and self-aware ▶
